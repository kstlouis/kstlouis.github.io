I found some research somewhere that you are more likely to learn things by teaching them to someone else than you are to just read and/or write about them. (If I ever find that again, I'll link to it). Assuming that isn't total garbage, I decided that while I was taking my web development bootcamp I would write out my notes as if I was demoing to someone else; taking the learn-by-doing approach a step further.

In theory, I'll actually retain more of what I'm learning this way. If you're really bored (and/or completely insane) you are welcome to follow along and see my "notes" get progressively better-looking as time goes on and I learn more things.
